# Billing FAQ

## What plans does AcmeSaaS offer?

AcmeSaaS offers three plans:

- **Free**: Up to 5 users, 3 projects, 100 MB storage. No integrations or SSO.
- **Pro**: $12/user/month (billed annually) or $15/user/month (billed monthly). Unlimited projects, 10 GB storage per user, Slack and GitHub integrations, Google and Microsoft SSO, priority email support.
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing. Everything in Pro plus SAML SSO, audit logs, custom roles, dedicated support manager, 99.9% uptime SLA, unlimited storage, and advanced analytics.

## How do I upgrade my plan?

1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select the new plan
4. Enter payment information (if not already on file)
5. Confirm the upgrade

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.

## How do I add or remove users from my plan?

Your bill adjusts automatically when you add or remove users. Adding a user mid-cycle charges a prorated amount. Removing a user mid-cycle provides a prorated credit applied to your next invoice.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and wire transfer (Enterprise plans only). All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

## Can I get an invoice?

Yes. Invoices are generated automatically on each billing date and available under Settings > Billing > Invoices. Enterprise customers can request custom invoicing with NET 30 payment terms.

## What happens if my payment fails?

If a payment fails, we retry 3 times over 7 days. During this period, your workspace remains fully functional. If payment is not resolved after 7 days, your workspace is downgraded to the Free plan. Your data is preserved for 30 days, after which it may be deleted.

## Do you offer annual billing discounts?

Yes. Annual billing is 20% cheaper than monthly billing. Pro annual is $12/user/month (vs $15/month on monthly billing). Enterprise plans are always billed annually.

## Can I get a refund?

Refund eligibility depends on your plan:
- **Monthly plans**: No refunds for partial months. You can cancel anytime and your plan remains active until the end of the billing cycle.
- **Annual plans**: Full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, prorated refunds are available for the remaining unused months.
- **Enterprise plans**: Refund terms are specified in your enterprise agreement. Contact your account manager for details.

## Is there a free trial?

Yes. All new workspaces get a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan. No credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to upgrade or continue on the Free plan.
