# Refund Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

## Overview

AcmeSaaS offers refunds based on your plan type and billing cycle. We want every customer to feel confident in their purchase.

## Monthly Plans

Monthly plans are billed at the start of each billing cycle. If you cancel during a cycle:
- Your workspace remains active until the end of the current billing period
- No partial refunds are issued for unused days within a billing cycle
- You can continue using all features until the cycle ends

## Annual Plans

Annual plans are billed once per year at a discounted rate.

### Within 30 Days of Purchase
- Full refund available, no questions asked
- Refund is processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method
- Contact support@acmesaas.com to request a refund

### After 30 Days
- Prorated refunds are available for the remaining unused months
- Prorated amount = (remaining full months / 12) x annual payment
- Partial months are not eligible for prorated refunds
- Refund is processed within 10 business days

## Enterprise Plans

Enterprise plan refund terms are defined in the individual enterprise agreement. In general:
- Service changes (such as feature modifications, API limit adjustments, or platform updates) do not qualify for refunds
- If a service change materially impacts your usage, contact your account manager to discuss options such as rate limit increases or plan modifications
- Refunds for enterprise plans require approval from the AcmeSaaS finance team

## Downgrades

If you downgrade from a paid plan to the Free plan:
- Your data is preserved, but features beyond the Free plan limits are disabled
- If you exceed Free plan limits (users, projects, storage), you'll need to reduce usage before the downgrade takes effect
- No refunds are issued for downgrades mid-cycle on monthly plans
- Annual plan downgrades follow the prorated refund policy above

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email support@acmesaas.com with the subject "Refund Request"
2. Include your workspace name and billing email
3. Our team will respond within 2 business days
4. Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

## Exceptions

- Refunds are not available for workspaces that have been terminated for Terms of Service violations
- Refunds are not available for charges incurred more than 90 days ago
- Add-on purchases (additional storage, premium support hours) are non-refundable after 14 days
