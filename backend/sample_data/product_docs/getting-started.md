# Getting Started with AcmeSaaS

Welcome to AcmeSaaS, the modern project management platform built for teams that ship fast.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit app.acmesaas.com and click "Get Started"
2. Enter your work email and create a password (minimum 10 characters, must include a number)
3. Verify your email through the confirmation link
4. You'll be prompted to create or join a workspace

## Setting Up Your Workspace

A workspace is the top-level container for your team. Each workspace has its own projects, members, and settings.

To create a workspace:
- Click "Create Workspace" after email verification
- Choose a workspace name (this becomes your URL: yourname.acmesaas.com)
- Select your plan (Free, Pro, or Enterprise)
- Invite team members via email

## Your First Project

Once your workspace is ready:

1. Click "New Project" from the dashboard
2. Choose a template (Kanban, Scrum, or Blank)
3. Name your project and add a description
4. Configure your workflow stages (default: To Do, In Progress, Review, Done)
5. Start creating tasks

## Navigation

- **Dashboard**: Overview of all your projects, recent activity, and assigned tasks
- **Projects**: List of all projects in your workspace
- **My Tasks**: Personal view of tasks assigned to you across all projects
- **Reports**: Built-in analytics and export options
- **Settings**: Workspace configuration, billing, and integrations

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Cmd/Ctrl + K`: Quick search across all projects
- `Cmd/Ctrl + N`: Create new task
- `Cmd/Ctrl + Shift + P`: Switch between projects
- `Cmd/Ctrl + .`: Toggle sidebar
- `Cmd/Ctrl + Enter`: Submit task or comment

## Mobile App

AcmeSaaS is available on iOS and Android. Download from the App Store or Google Play. The mobile app supports:
- Viewing and editing tasks
- Push notifications for assignments and mentions
- Offline mode with automatic sync
- Camera integration for attaching photos to tasks

## Getting Help

- In-app help: Click the "?" icon in the bottom-right corner
- Documentation: docs.acmesaas.com
- Support: support@acmesaas.com (Pro and Enterprise plans get priority support)
